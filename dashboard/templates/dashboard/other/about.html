Our Story

Founded in 2023, SmartBin began as a university project at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology with a simple goal: to make waste management more efficient through technology.

What started as a class project has grown into a comprehensive solution adopted by municipalities across India, reducing collection costs by up to 30% while increasing recycling rates.

Our team of engineers, environmentalists, and data scientists continue to innovate new ways to tackle urban waste challenges.
